Crockpot Italian Chicken

Description

Crockpot Italian Chicken is a simple, hearty dish perfect for busy days when you want something flavorful with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 (16-ounce) bottle of Italian dressingli>
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Quick Steps to Make

  1. Prepare Chicken: Place 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts in the bottom of your slow cooker.
  2. Add Dressing: Pour 1 (16-ounce) bottle of Italian dressing over the chicken. Make sure the chicken is coated evenly.
  3. Add Parmesan: Sprinkle ¼ cup of grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the chicken.
  4. Cook: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or until the chicken is tender and fully cooked.
  5. Serve: Once done, serve the Italian chicken with pasta, rice, or veggies of your choice!
