Spinach Ricotta Quiche

Description

A spinach ricotta quiche is a savory, comforting dish that combines the earthy richness of fresh spinach with the creamy, mild flavor of ricotta cheese, all encased in a buttery, flaky pie crust.

Ingredients

  • single-crust pie
  • butter
  • chopped red onion
  • fresh spinach
  • whole-milk ricotta cheese
  • heavy cream
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • large eggs
  • chopped fresh basil
  • salt
  • ground black pepper

Quick Steps to make

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).​
  2. Prepare Crust: Place a store-bought or homemade pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish.​
  3. Sauté Vegetables: In a skillet, sauté chopped onion and garlic in butter until translucent. Add thawed and drained frozen spinach; cook until any excess moisture evaporates.​
  4. Mix Filling: In a bowl, combine the sautéed spinach mixture with ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan, and beaten eggs. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg if desired.​
  5. Assemble Quiche: Pour the filling into the prepared pie crust, spreading it evenly.
  6. Bake: Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35 to 45 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden.​
  7. Cool and Serve: Allow the quiche to cool for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
